Game Theme:

	1 Bodies and spirits, particularly "out of body" experiences.
	2 Maintaining Relationships.
		As subthemes to both 1 and 2: Freedom versus Power. Maintaining what you own responsibly.
	Mazes.

Other details:
	-The main character is a spirit who can freely leave and reposess a body.
	-In spirit form, they are free from collisions and can walk through walls and over pits.
		Nothing can hurt the spirit, and they are free.
	-In their body, they exchange freedom from the physcial world for the power to interact with it.
	-They also need to maintain their body.
		The goal is not to become more power, but rather to maintain the things which give them their power.
	-The main character's initial goal is to navigate their body outside of a labrynth. 
		They can leave their body to gain information and perspectives in spirit form which they could not obtain relying soley on their body.

Setting:
	A fantasy multifloor labrynth under the sea floor. Many fantasy creatures dwell in it.

Inhabitants:
	Mainly merfolk, dwarves, and possibly goblins.
	Also homunculi, automaton, and undead variants of the above.
	(Undead as in skeletons, zombies, vampires, and ghosts).
	Maybe a dragon or similary sized sea monster as a floor boss?
	Sharks, dolphins, or whales? Jellyfish?
	Another spirit acts as the player's guide.

Abilities:
	-In spirit form, the other player has the ability to see other spirits.
		However, the player needs to keep these senses sharp by maintaining their friendship with their spirit guide. Should they lose this gift, they will not get it back.
	-The spirits can pass through walls freely to explore.
		In this state, however, the physical world slowly fades from view, so they must act quickly to gain the information they want.
	-The spirits can possess other unoccupied bodies besides their own (particularly homunculi and automatons).
		Possessing bodies belonging to someone else will bring negative long term consequences though.
	-Open doors.
	-Press levers.

Potential abilities:
	-Talk - talk with NPCs.
	-Alchemy - With the right materials, you could construct a homunculus?
	-Magic - command the elements for help with combat and puzzle obstacles.
	-???Glasses?Crystal Ball? - a technique to allow the soul to see things from a distance?
	-Swimming?

Key benchmarks for the player's journey:
	Each will open new paths but close old ones in the process, restructuring the maze.
	-Find a way to light up the labyrnth. This improves navigation and awakens native inhabitants.
	-Drain the seawater from the labrynth for new access ways.
	-Repair airlocks and pressure systems. Doing so blocks airvent access but opens formerly unsurvivable locations.
	-Restore electricity to the labrynth. This opens certain elevators but reactivates automated security systems.
	-Open the main gates.
